Yes — first-timers often have the deepest experiences because they arrive with no preconceptions. You will be guided every step of the way. The only requirement is a quiet space, headphones, and an open mind.

No. Hypnosis is a focused state of heightened awareness — not unconsciousness. You remain in complete control throughout. You cannot be made to say or do anything against your will. Think of it as an intensely relaxed, focused state — like being absorbed in a great book.
